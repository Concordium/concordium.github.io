.. admonition:: At a glance

   This guide walks you through installing all the tools needed to follow the Concordium smart contract tutorials. You will need a Chrome browser and internet access. When you are done, you will have Rust, cargo-concordium, and concordium-client installed, a funded testnet wallet set up, and your account key imported into concordium-client — ready to build, deploy, and interact with smart contracts on testnet.
