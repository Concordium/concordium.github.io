==============
Desktop Wallet
==============

The Concordium Desktop Wallet is a digital wallet that enables you to create and manage your Concordium identities, credentials, and accounts, and to create transactions such as sending CCD, adding a baker, and exporting and importing account information. The Desktop Wallet also allows for multi signature accounts.

The Desktop Wallet also supports storing of your private keys on a LEDGER device.
